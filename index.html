About me

I'm originally from Brazil and right before I moved to the United States in 2009, I graduated with a Title of Technologist in Computer Science in Business Management from FATEC, a São Paulo state Technological College. I came to the US to be an Au Pair and improve my English and ended up getting an Associate’s Degree in Graphic Design from Foothill College in California. I later joined Wix.com as a customer support agent and worked there for 5 years before leaving to do a web-development coding Bootcamp full-time course at Ironhack Miami.

I live in Miami with my husband and our 4 cats, other than enjoying coding and designing websites, I love running, pilates, dancing, and all sorts of physical activities. I also love going to concerts and travelling, I'm always up for a new adventure, whether it is trying out a new dish or swimming with sharks.
